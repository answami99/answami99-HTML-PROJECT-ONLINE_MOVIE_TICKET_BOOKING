Baazaar

Discription

Baazaar is an upcoming 2018 crime film directed by Gauravv K. Chawla, written by Nikkhil Advani, Aseem Arora and Parveez Sheikh. Starring Saif Ali Khan and debutant Rohan Mehra, Chitrangada Singh and Radhika Apte. Film is set in the backdrop of Money, Power & Business. largely based on the Stock market.

Cast

Saif Ali Khan as Nawaab Rohan Mehra as Gaurav Chitrangda Singh as Gayatri Denzil Smith as Nawab's Enemy Radhika Apte as Gaurav's Mother Atul Kulkarni as Nawab's Bodyguard Saurabh Shukla as Mota Pawan Chopra as Zulfikar Anupriya Goenka as Nawab's sister
