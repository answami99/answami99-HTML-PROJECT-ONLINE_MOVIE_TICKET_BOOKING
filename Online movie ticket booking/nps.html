Naa Peru Surya

Discription

Naa Peru Surya, Naa Illu India (lit.?My name is Surya, My home is India) is a 2018 Telugu-language action film produced by Sirisha and Sridhar Lagadapati under the Ramalakshmi Cine Creations banner, written and directed by Vakkantham Vamsi.
